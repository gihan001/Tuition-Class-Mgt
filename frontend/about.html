About Our System

The Smart Tuition Class Management System is a modern web platform designed to make the tuition industry more efficient and digitalized. It simplifies data management by connecting students, teachers, and administrators (Admins) into a single network.

Vision

To elevate Sri Lanka's tuition sector to the highest level through technology.

Mission

To provide software facilities that allow students and teachers to easily manage classroom activities.
